The 7 Deadly Mistakes

1

Checking Domains One by One

You open GoDaddy. Type a domain. Wait. Check availability. Repeat. By the time you've checked 10 domains, someone else bought the good ones. Time cost: 3-4 hours per day.

2

Trusting "Looks Good" Instead of Metrics

A domain "sounds nice" so you buy it. But you didn't check backlinks, authority, or history. Result: You own a pretty name with zero value. Money lost: $50-$500 per mistake.

3

Missing the Window

Good expired domains get bought within minutes of dropping. If you're not checking constantly, you're missing 90% of opportunities. Opportunity cost: Unlimited.

4

No System for Tracking Finds

You found a great domain last Tuesday. Where did you write it down? Which notebook? Which app? By the time you find your notes, it's sold. Domains lost: Countless.

5

Paying for Multiple Tools

SpamZilla subscription. Domain research tool. Backlink checker. Availability API. You're spending $200+/month on tools that don't talk to each other. Monthly waste: $150+.

6

No Monetization Strategy

You buy domains hoping to flip them. But what if they don't sell? You need affiliate intelligence, market research, and backup monetization plans. Without this, you're gambling.

7

Doing Everything Manually

The pros use systems. They check hundreds of domains daily. They buy with data. They sell with confidence. You're doing everything by hand. That's why they're winning and you're not.
